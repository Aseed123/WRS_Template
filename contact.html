Reliable IT & Printing Solutions That Power Your Business.

WRS Office Automation Trading LLC (OPC) was established in 2023 as a proud sister company of White Rose Stationery LLC, with a strong vision to deliver reliable, efficient, and future-ready office solutions across the UAE. Built on the core values of trust, quality, and customer satisfaction, WRS specializes in end-to-end office automation and workplace support services designed to meet the evolving demands of modern businesses. Our comprehensive offerings include the sales and servicing of new and refurbished photocopiers, original and compatible toners, stationery products, IT accessories, and genuine printer spare parts. We work closely with enterprises, institutions, and growing businesses to ensure uninterrupted operations, optimized performance, and long-term cost efficiency. Partnering with globally recognized brands such as Canon, Ricoh, HP, Kyocera, Brother, and many more, we guarantee products and solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability. With a strong focus on affordability, quality assurance, and responsive customer care, WRS Office Automation Trading LLC continues to grow as a trusted and dependable name in office automation and supply solutions throughout the UAE.
